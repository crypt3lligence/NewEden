NEW EDEN

Well into the 21st century, a fusion of biotech, cybernetics and AI became all but inevitable.

The Cyberverse - a colloquial name for a ubiquitous, decentralized virtual infrastructure – has become intertwined in almost every imaginable aspect of human existance. It is told that some of the earliest believers in such technology were able to harness its powers to achieve the unimaginable. They sought ways to enhance their bodies, prolong their lives and integrate their being into the virtual networks.

Thus, the first “augmented” humans were generated. In time, they became referred to as the Originals of New Eden. Their story begins here.

CELINE

A.k.a “code: 0xymoron” (in the more hidden corners of the Cyberverse), Celine is a Metasurfer – a class of augmented humans known for their advanced abilities in integrating and traversing the virtual systems.

Frequently diving into the depths of the Cyberverse void, she’s an adept hacker and a street-smart Edenite.

ARGARION

Little is documented about an entity known as Argarion. Akin to a mythical tale, some believe he was once a renowned robotics engineer who managed to resurrect his decaying body by fusing it with the most advanced cyberwear.

Rumour has it that a new cult recently emerged, devoted to an obscure figure who “rose from the void”.
